Culture Sharing, Fun Events and Learning Experience

Exchange of Cultural values is gist of the formation of the club. We aim to have free talks and discussions on various aspects of diverse cultures from across the globe. We aim to expose the members as well as guests to different languages. We thrive due to diversity and we as a club celebrate the diversity of the Stevens Community.
